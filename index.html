Our Story

Founded in 2004 by Yakub Omar, a former health services civil servant, Nagelle set out to deliver quality healthcare, build a modern pharmaceutical brand, create jobs, and support underprivileged communities.

Today, with a team of 30, we continue to expand our services, improve community health, and stay true to our mission of making a meaningful difference in the lives we serve.

What We Do

Nagelle Drugmart and Agrovet provides high-quality pharmaceutical and veterinary products to promote the health of people and animals.

We offer personalized services for farmers, pet owners, and healthcare professionals, building lasting relationships as a trusted partner in health and wellness.

Our Mission

High-quality Healthcare and Veterinary Products

Our mission at Nagelle Drugmart and Agrovet is to provide high-quality healthcare and veterinary products that enhance the well-being of our community. We are committed to delivering exceptional service, fostering job creation, and ensuring accessibility to health resources for all, particularly for underprivileged members of society. Through education and innovation, we strive to empower our customers with the knowledge they need for better health management.

Our Vision

Leading Provider of Pharmaceutical & Agrovet services in the region.

Our vision is to be the leading provider of pharmaceutical and agrovet services in the region, recognized for our commitment to quality, integrity, and community welfare. We aim to build a sustainable future by promoting health and wellness for both people and animals, while continuously expanding our reach and impact within the communities we serve.
